Ecology and zero waste

Our ambition is zero waste production. Therefore, we have
minimized waste generated during raw material processing,
recycling it into products such as pellets and
animal bedding. All of this is done out of respect for wood,
considering dwindling natural resources, and with a commitment
to the future generations.

Passion and innovation

We are well acquainted with the
industry and understand the challenges of sourcing
high-quality straws. We decided to create a product initially
dedicated to our horses, and now we want to share it with you as well.
Our animal bedding is an ecological and hypoallergenic product born out
of a passion for breeding and a commitment to the well-being of animals .
